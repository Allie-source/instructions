Color-Linez

The game is very simple.

  • You must first click on an empty square for the first colored dots to appear
  • There will be various colors that you must match 5 or more of in a row then they will disappear
  • To move the colored dots around click on a color and then click to the empty square you would like to move it to
  • You can only move a color to a block that has an unobstructed view of the colors block(no colors blocking it)
  • There will not be multiples of 5 of every color
  • Clear the board to win the game
